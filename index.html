About Us

At the Virtual Joint Forces Command, we emphasize rigorous training programs designed to enhance pilot proficiency across various aircraft and mission scenarios. Our members engage in realistic simulations that cover everything from basic flight maneuvers to advanced tactical operations. Whether you're honing your skills in air-to-air combat, practicing formation flying, or conducting complex mission planning, our structured training modules ensure that every pilot can improve and excel.

Units

The Virtual Joint Forces Command was activated in late 2024 with support from the US Navy, US Air Force, and US Army. The command is headquartered at the redesignated Joint Base Nellis-Creech, additionally the 23AF and 1CAB have also completed their change of stations to Nellis AFB and Fort Creech.

CSG-3

Carrier Strike Group 3

Carrier Strike Group 3 was reactivated and assigned to the vJFCOM in the Fall of 2024 as a quick reaction force capable of global response and direct action to support national interests within 48 hours. Due to ongoing reactivation of virtual CSG's and crowded conditions at traditional home ports for carrier groups, the 3rd is based out of NB Guam after significant upgrades were made to the base. Supplemented by the Marines Aviation Combat Element of the 33rd MEU, II MEF.

23AF

23rd Air Forces

Supporting USAF ACC in virtual theaters globally, elements from the 23AF provide rapid response and strike capabilties through a combination of advanced flight training and coordinated mission planning. In preperation for upcoming deployments, AFSOC has attached the reactivated 932d Air Control Squadron to provide critical airborne command and control capabilities.

1 CAB

1st Combat Aviation Brigade

The 1st CAB is equipped to conduct operations in diverse environments, from direct combat in conflict zones to humanitarian and peacekeeping missions of the U.S. Army, specializing in aviation support across a variety of missions, including attack, reconnaissance, air assault, transport. and critical personnel recovery making us the favorites of several of the CSG and 23rd pilots.
