Requirements

New Enlistments upon approval will enter into the Training Pipleine according to their applied Occupation (MOS/AFSC/Rating) and are required to complete their evaluations/checkrides prior to the end of the probationary period. Slots for open Occupations will be listed below or can be found on the ORBAT. Students will enter their unit upon graduation and recieve their promotion and decorations. Find out more about your Occupation of Interest's requirements on Discord.

Active Members are required to complete xx number of hours in weekly coordinated in-game training, accoridng to their unit's ongoing training curiculumn and currency requirements. These hours can be completed at any time, with any qualified wingmen, and must be logged prior to Sunday night each week.
